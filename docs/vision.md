# STFC Visual Intelligence Platform

## Mission
Create a modern visual intelligence platform for Star Trek Fleet Command (STFC) that transforms complex ship data into engaging, intuitive, and highly visual experiences.

The platform should prioritize visual understanding over spreadsheets, tables, and dense textual explanations.

Many STFC players have access to large amounts of information but do not engage deeply with traditional data-heavy tools. The platform should focus on making information immediately understandable through animation, visualization, interaction, and storytelling.

The goal is not simply to answer:

"What are the stats?"

The goal is to answer:

"What does this ship do, how does it feel, and why should I care?"

---

## Product Philosophy

### Visual First
Whenever possible, show rather than tell.

Animations, visual timelines, battle rhythms, progression maps, and interactive comparisons should be preferred over large tables.

### Substance Before Decoration
Visuals must communicate meaningful information.

Animations should reflect actual game mechanics whenever possible.

For example:

- Weapon firing patterns
- Combat timing
- Damage cadence
- Progression paths
- Resource investments

Visuals should not be cosmetic; they should help players understand the game.

### Modular Growth
The platform will be developed as a collection of small, focused projects rather than a single large application.

Each project should provide standalone value while contributing to a larger ecosystem.

### Shared Foundations
All applications should consume the same underlying ship, combat, and asset models.

Business logic should be reusable across applications.

---

## Long-Term Product Areas

### Ship Animator
Visualize ship firing patterns, weapon timing, recoil, projectiles, and combat rhythm.

### Ship Explorer
Browse and discover ships through rich visual interfaces.

### Ship Comparator
Compare ships through visualizations rather than raw tables.

### Combat Simulator
Simulate and visualize battles between ships.

### Progression Explorer
Visualize progression paths, upgrades, successors, and alternatives.

### Investment Analyzer
Evaluate resource efficiency and long-term value.

### Account Advisor
Provide account-aware recommendations based on player progression.

---

## Initial Development Strategy
Development should begin with a highly visual project that demonstrates value quickly.

The first application will be the Ship Animator.

The Ship Animator is intentionally narrow in scope and serves as a proving ground for:

- Shared ship models
- Shared combat models
- Asset management
- Animation systems
- UI standards

Lessons learned from the Ship Animator will inform future applications.
